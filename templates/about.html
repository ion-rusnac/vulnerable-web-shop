About PrintyToys

We are passionate about 3D printing and bringing imagination to life. From articulated dragons to customized miniatures, our toys are crafted with precision, quality, and a touch of magic.

Our goal is to provide a premium experience for collectors, tabletop enthusiasts, and kids alike. Everything you see in our shop is printed using eco-friendly PLA plastics and high-resolution resin printers for those intricate details.

Our Process

  • Design: We start with incredible digital sculpts.
  • Print: Utilizing state-of-the-art FDM and SLA printers.
  • Quality Check: Every model is inspected and post-processed by hand.
